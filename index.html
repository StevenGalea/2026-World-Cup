⚙️ How Scores Update

Open scores.json on GitHub

Go to github.com/StevenGalea/2026-World-Cup and tap scores.json. Works fine in mobile Safari/Chrome.

Tap the pencil ✏️ to edit

Find the team that played and update their points (e.g. a win in GS1 = change "GS1":0 to "GS1":3). Also update "lastUpdated" at the top with today's date.

Commit changes

Scroll down → tap Commit changes. GitHub Pages rebuilds in ~20-30 seconds.

Everyone refreshes

Hit ↻ Refresh on the dashboard. No accounts, no Google Sheets, nothing to connect.
