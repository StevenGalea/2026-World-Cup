The sheet has columns: Team | GS1 | GS2 | GS3 | Rd32 | Rd16 | QF | SF | 3rd | Final | Bonus

Publish the sheet as CSV

In your copy: File → Share → Publish to web
Set Sheet1 and Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish.
Copy the URL it gives you — it looks like:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID/pub?gid=0&single=true&output=csv
Paste your published CSV URL here

The URL is saved in your browser. Anyone using this dashboard on the same device stays connected automatically.

Update scores from your iPhone

Open the Google Sheet on your phone via the Google Sheets app or Safari. Edit any score cell and save. Hit ↻ Refresh on the dashboard and scores pull through instantly. No coding required.
